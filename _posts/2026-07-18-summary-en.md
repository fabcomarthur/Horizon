## [Google Claims Billions of AI Search Clicks, Lacks Verifiable Data](https://www.searchenginejournal.com/google-puts-a-number-on-ai-search-clicks-without-the-data/582755/) ⭐️ 7.0/10

Google's Nick Fox stated that AI features in Search are driving billions of clicks to websites weekly, a figure presented without any verifiable data or methodology to support the claim. This lack of transparency from Google is significant for SEO professionals and website owners who rely on accurate data to understand traffic sources and measure the impact of AI on organic search performance, raising concerns about attribution and effective strategy. The claim of billions of weekly clicks from AI features is presented alongside Google's existing daily click count, neither of which can be independently audited or verified by external parties.
